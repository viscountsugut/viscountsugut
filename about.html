About MeData

MeData is an organisation that provides both the patients and professionals within the medical fraternity the platform of recording and retrieval of vital medical records for diagnosis. The platform is also useful for researchers and statisticians who tend to find a hard time in accessing medical records for evaluation. Transfer of patients between hospitals, both locally and internationally is covered as MeData stores patients medical records on an online database.

MeData intends to attract a gobal audience and with ease of sharing of personal data to licenced medical facilities, the patient will have the laxity of accesssing professional consulation without necessarily travelling to the preferred centers. The platform will also provide the much-needed transfer of records within healthcare institution across the globe.

Founder of MeData

MeData Was started by Sugut Viscount, a renowned graduate Engineer from Kenya. Accordng to Sugut, the inspiration behing MeData is the constant loss of data among patients and the vulnerabilty of patients to misdiagnossis often associated with lack of Patients Medical records.
Equally, the frustration that arises with transfer of patients within hospitals,both to the doctors and patients, is key in design of MeData.
Having made contact with several patients who have suffered due to lack of proper medical recording as well as patients strugling with rerieval of data, including CT Scan files and MRI Images, Sugut formulated an web-based platform that provides vital soluton to the challenges and at the same time enhance the ease of access to data for researchers as well as enhancing platforms for global consultation servises.
